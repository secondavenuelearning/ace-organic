Administration Tool

The administrator verifies instructors' identities, a step that is necessary before an instructor can create courses, etc. The administrator must also actively grant an instructor permission to author as either a local or a master author. ACE stores the permission in users_v4.flags.

The administrator also has the ability to edit user profiles, to delete users, and to impersonate other instructors or students. The administrator is able to force ACE to reload the systemwide constants (functional group definitions, formatting rules, etc.) that it loads from files upon start-up.

The administrator may confer translator privileges on selected instructors.

An ACE administrator can post a system notice that appears on users' home pages after they log in. The administrator should log in as the owner of the Tutorials course, enter that course, and enter the system notice as the course notes.
