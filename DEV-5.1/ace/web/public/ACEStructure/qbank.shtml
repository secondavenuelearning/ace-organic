Question Bank

When an instructor presses the Question Bank, ACE opens authortool/enterAuthortool.jsp, which determines whether the instructor is a master or local author (see below) and then delivers the instructor to authortool/questionsList.jsp, the question set display page.

Instructors are divided into master and local authors. Any additions or changes to the database made by local authors are visible only to that instructor in all of her courses, and not to any other instructor. (However, a coinstructor of a course may assign his or her work to the course's owner.) The administrator must choose to grant local or master authoring privileges to each instructor. A master author may choose to act as a local author if he or she wants to write questions that are not visible to any other instructors.
